CINNABONS

Ingredients for 12 rolls:

    Dough:

  • Flour .... 520 - 550 g
  • Milk (about 38 degrees) .... 240 ml
  • Dry yeast ... 7 g
  • Eggs (large) ... 2 pcs.
  • Sugar ... 80 g
  • Unsalted Butter .... 80 g
  • Salt ... Â½ tsp
  • Vanilla Extract ... 1 tsp

    • Filling:

  • Unsalted Butter (soft) ... 100 g
  • Cane sugar .... 100-120 g
  • Cinnamon ... 15 g

    • Glaze:

  • Whipping Cream ... 120 g
  • Powdered Sugar ... 80 g

Cooking steps

Step 1

Dissolve yeast in warm milk. Add 1 teaspoon of sugar. And leave for 10 minutes

Step 2

In the meantime, break the eggs into a bowl, add salt, sugar, vanilla extract, mix, add the yeast that has come up, add part of the flour, melted butter and the remaining flour. The dough should be quite soft and tender. Knead the dough until it stops sticking to the table - 5-7minutes.


Step 3

Transfer the dough to a bowl, cover with cling film and leave in a warm place for 1 hour.

Step 4

Prepare the filling: mix sugar with cinnamon.


Step 5

Cover the form with parchment paper and grease with vegetable oil.


Step 6

Roll out the dough into a rectangular layer, grease the entire layer with butter at room temperature.


Step 7

Sprinkle the sugar and cinnamon in an even layer over the butter.


Step 8

Gently roll into a roll. Divide the roll with a thread into 12 parts.


Step 9

Arrange the buns on the baking sheet close enough to each other.

Cover with cling film and let the buns rest for 40 minutes.

Step 10

Send to the oven with a temperature of 180 degrees Celsius for 25 minutes

Step 11

Mix powdered sugar with cream - prepare a creamy glaze. Take the buns out of the oven. Cover hot buns with cream glaze

Bon appetit
